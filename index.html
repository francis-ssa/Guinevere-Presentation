My Build for Guinevere

Battle Spell and Emblems

Strengths

  • High Burst Damage: Capable of eliminating squishy targets quickly
  • Crowd Control Abilities: Effective in team fights with her knock-up skills
  • Mobility: Can reposition quickly, making her hard to catch
  • Early Game Dominance: Strong presence in early skirmishes

Weaknesses

  • Vulnerability: requires good positioning to avoid being caught
  • Skill Dependent: needs precise timing to maximize damage potential
  • Immune dash heroes (Benedetta, Chou, etc)
  • Immune skill heroes (Hanabi, Wanwan, etc)
  • Purify spell

Gameplay Tips

  • Use Bushes for Ambushes: Hide in the bushes before engaging enemies
  • Prioritize Backline Targets: Focus on enemy mages and marksmen during team fights
  • Engage After Enemy CC is Burned: Wait for enemy crowd control abilities before diving in
